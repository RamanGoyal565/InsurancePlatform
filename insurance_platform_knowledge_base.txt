INSURANCE PLATFORM — CUSTOMER HELP GUIDE

SECTION 1: WHAT IS INSURANCE PLATFORM?
----------------------------------------------------------------

Insurance Platform is an online service where you can buy,
manage, and renew vehicle insurance — all from your phone or
computer, without visiting any office.

We cover three types of vehicles:
- Cars
- Trucks
- Bikes

What you can do on the platform:
- Browse and compare insurance plans
- Buy a policy and get covered instantly
- View and download your policy document
- Renew your policy when it is about to expire
- File a claim if something happens to your vehicle
- Raise a support request if you need help
- Track your payments
- Get instant answers from our AI assistant

SECTION 2: CREATING AN ACCOUNT
----------------------------------------------------------------

SIGNING UP
To create an account, go to the Sign Up page and enter:
- Your full name
- Your email address
- A password (at least 8 characters)

After signing up, we will send a 6-digit verification code
to your email. Enter this code to activate your account.
The code is valid for 10 minutes.

LOGGING IN
Go to the Login page and enter your email and password.
If your details are wrong, you will see an error message
right away — the page will not refresh or clear your input.

FORGOT YOUR PASSWORD?
1. Click "Forgot password?" on the Login page.
2. Enter your email address.
3. We will send a 6-digit code to your email.
4. Enter the code when prompted.
5. Choose a new password (at least 8 characters).
6. Log in with your new password.

The code expires in 10 minutes. If you did not receive it,
wait 60 seconds and click "Resend OTP".

KEEPING YOUR ACCOUNT SAFE
- Use a strong password that you do not use elsewhere.
- Your session will automatically end after 2 hours.
- We will never ask for your password by email or phone.
- If you think someone else accessed your account, raise
  a support ticket immediately.

SECTION 4: BUYING A POLICY
----------------------------------------------------------------

HOW TO BUY
1. Go to "Browse Policies" from the menu.
2. Find the plan you want and click "Buy Policy".
3. Enter your vehicle registration number
   (example: DL01AB1234).
4. Enter your driving licence number
   (example: DL1420150001234).
5. Check the summary — it shows the plan name, your vehicle
   details, and the total amount.
6. Click "Pay via Razorpay" to go to the payment page.
7. Complete your payment.
8. Your policy is active immediately after payment.

PAYMENT OPTIONS
We use Razorpay for secure payments. You can pay using:
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and others)
- Credit or Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- Net Banking
- Wallets

ONE VEHICLE, ONE POLICY
Each vehicle can only have one active policy at a time.
If you already have an active policy for a vehicle and try
to buy another one for the same vehicle, you will see an
error message. You need to wait until the current policy
expires or is cancelled before buying a new one.

AFTER PAYMENT
Once your payment goes through:
- Your policy is active right away.
- A receipt appears on screen with your payment details.
- You can download the receipt as a file.
- Your policy appears in "My Policies".

WHAT IF PAYMENT FAILS?
If your payment does not go through, no policy is created.
You can simply try again — go back to "Browse Policies"
and start the purchase again.

SECTION 5: YOUR POLICIES
----------------------------------------------------------------

WHERE TO SEE YOUR POLICIES
Go to "My Policies" from the menu. You will see a list of
all the policies you have purchased, showing:
- Policy number
- Plan name
- Your vehicle registration number
- Your driving licence number
- Vehicle type
- Current status
- Expiry date
- Action button (Renew or Buy Again)

POLICY STATUS EXPLAINED
- Active: Your policy is valid and you are covered.
- Renewed: Your policy was renewed and is still valid.
- Pending: Your payment is being processed. This usually
  resolves within a few seconds.
- Expired: Your policy has passed its end date.
- Cancelled: Your policy was cancelled, usually because
  a payment did not go through.

DOWNLOADING YOUR POLICY DOCUMENT
Click the document icon next to any policy in "My Policies"
to open the PDF viewer. From there, click "Download" to
save the document to your device.

SECTION 6: RENEWING YOUR POLICY
----------------------------------------------------------------

WHEN CAN YOU RENEW?
You can renew your policy starting from 30 days before it
expires, and up to 15 days after it expires.

For example, if your policy expires on 15 August:
- You can renew from 15 July onwards.
- The last day to renew is 30 August.

If you try to renew too early (more than 30 days before
expiry), you will see a message asking you to wait.

HOW TO RENEW
1. Go to "My Policies".
2. Click "Renew" next to the policy you want to renew.
3. Complete the payment.
4. Your policy is extended for another year.

WHAT IF MY POLICY HAS ALREADY EXPIRED?
You still have 15 days after the expiry date to renew.
After those 15 days, renewal is no longer possible.

In that case, the button will show "Buy Again" instead of
"Renew". Click it to purchase a fresh policy for the same
vehicle.

CAN I CANCEL MY POLICY?
Cancellation through the customer portal is not currently
available. If you need to cancel, please raise a Support
Ticket and our team will assist you.

SECTION 7: FILING A CLAIM
----------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT IS A CLAIM?
A claim is a request for compensation when something covered
by your policy happens — for example, an accident, theft,
or damage to your vehicle.

HOW TO FILE A CLAIM
1. Go to "Tickets & Claims" from the menu.
2. Click "Raise New Ticket".
3. Choose "Claim" as the type.
4. Select the policy the claim is for (if applicable).
5. Write a clear title for your claim.
6. Describe what happened in detail.
7. Enter the amount you are claiming.
8. Attach a supporting document (PDF, max 1 MB) — optional
   but recommended.
9. Click "Submit Ticket".

WHAT TO INCLUDE IN YOUR DESCRIPTION
The more detail you provide, the faster your claim can be
reviewed. Include:
- When and where the incident happened
- What happened (accident, theft, damage, etc.)
- The extent of damage or loss
- Whether a police report was filed (and the report number)
- Any other parties involved
- Your estimated repair or replacement cost

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU SUBMIT?
1. Your claim is received and assigned to a Claims Specialist.
2. The specialist reviews your claim and any documents.
3. You will receive a notification when a decision is made.
4. The decision is either Approved or Rejected.

Once a decision is made, it is final and cannot be changed.

CLAIM APPROVED
If your claim is approved, the settlement will be processed.
You will receive a notification confirming the approval.

CLAIM REJECTED
If your claim is rejected, you will receive a notification
explaining the outcome. If you believe the decision was
incorrect, please raise a new Support Ticket.

CHECKING YOUR CLAIM STATUS
Go to "Tickets & Claims" and click the eye icon next to
your claim to see the full details, including the current
status, specialist comments, and any attached documents.

SECTION 8: SUPPORT TICKETS
----------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT IS A SUPPORT TICKET?
A support ticket is how you ask for help with anything that
is not a claim — for example:
- Questions about your policy
- Help with a payment
- Account issues
- Technical problems
- Any other question or concern

HOW TO RAISE A SUPPORT TICKET
1. Go to "Tickets & Claims" from the menu.
2. Click "Raise New Ticket".
3. Choose "Support" as the type.
4. Select a related policy if relevant.
5. Write a clear title.
6. Describe your issue in detail.
7. Attach a document (PDF, max 1 MB) if it helps explain
   your issue — optional.
8. Click "Submit Ticket".

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?
Your ticket is reviewed by our team and assigned to a
Support Specialist. You will receive a notification when
someone is assigned and when they respond.

ADDING MORE INFORMATION
After submitting a ticket, you can add comments to provide
more details or respond to the specialist. You can also
attach one PDF document per comment.

TICKET STATUS EXPLAINED
- Open: Your ticket has been received.
- Assigned: A specialist has been assigned to help you.
- In Review: The specialist is looking into your issue.
- Resolved: Your issue has been resolved.
- Closed: The ticket has been closed.

VIEWING TICKET DETAILS
Click the eye icon next to any ticket to see the full
conversation, status, and any attached documents.

SECTION 9: PAYMENTS
----------------------------------------------------------------

VIEWING YOUR PAYMENT HISTORY
Go to "Payments" from the menu to see all your transactions,
including the amount, date, and whether the payment was
successful.

WHAT IF A PAYMENT FAILS?
If a payment fails, no policy is created or renewed.
You will receive a notification. Simply try again by
going back to "Browse Policies" or "My Policies".

PAYMENT RECEIPT
After every successful payment, a receipt is shown on screen.
You can download it as a file. Keep this receipt as proof
of payment — it includes a unique payment reference number.

YOUR PAYMENT INFORMATION IS SAFE
We do not store your card or bank details. All payments
are handled securely by Razorpay. We only keep a record
of the payment amount and reference number.

WHAT NOTIFICATIONS WILL I RECEIVE?

- Welcome message when you register

About your policies:

notifications you have. Click it to go to the Notifications

Only PDF files can be uploaded.

SECTION 12: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------

Q: How do I know if my policy is active?
A: Go to "My Policies". If the status shows "Active" or
   "Renewed", you are covered.

Q: Can I have two policies for the same vehicle?
A: No. Each vehicle can only have one active policy at a time.

Q: What should I do if my payment failed?
A: Simply try again. Go to "Browse Policies" to buy a new
   policy, or go to "My Policies" and click "Renew" to
   retry a renewal.

Q: How long does it take to process a claim?
A: Processing time depends on the complexity of your claim.
   You will receive notifications at every step so you
   always know what is happening.

Q: Can I cancel my policy?
A: Cancellation through the portal is not currently available.
   Please raise a Support Ticket and our team will help you.

Q: What happens if I miss the renewal window?
A: If more than 15 days have passed since your policy expired,
   you cannot renew it. Use the "Buy Again" button to purchase
   a new policy for your vehicle.

Q: Can I change my vehicle details after buying a policy?
A: Vehicle details cannot be changed after purchase. Please
   raise a Support Ticket if you need assistance.

Q: Is my payment information stored?
A: No. Your card or bank details are never stored on our
   platform. All payments are handled by Razorpay.

Q: How do I download my policy document?
A: Go to "My Policies" and click the document icon next to
   the policy. Click "Download" in the viewer.

Q: What is the difference between a Support Ticket and a Claim?
A: A Support Ticket is for general help and questions.
   A Claim is a formal request for insurance compensation
   after an incident like an accident or theft.

Q: Can I attach multiple documents to a claim?
A: You can attach one PDF when filing the ticket, and one
   more PDF with each comment you add afterwards.

Q: How do I contact a support agent?
A: Raise a Support Ticket from "Tickets & Claims". A
   specialist will be assigned and will respond through
   the comments.

Q: What vehicles are covered?
A: Cars, Trucks, and Bikes (motorcycles and scooters).

Q: How do I verify my email?
A: After registering, enter the 6-digit code sent to your
   email. If you did not receive it, click "Resend OTP"
   after 60 seconds.

Q: What should I include in a claim?
A: Date, time, and location of the incident; what happened;
   extent of damage; police report number if applicable;
   and your estimated cost.

Q: How will I know when my claim is decided?
A: You will receive a notification in the app and by email.

Q: Can I reopen a rejected claim?
A: No. Once a claim is decided, it is final. If you believe
   it was incorrect, raise a new Support Ticket.

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Use "Forgot Password" on the Login page. Enter your email,
   receive a code, verify it, and set a new password.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit Cards, Debit
   Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets — all through Razorpay.

Q: How do I know if the AI assistant is working?
A: The chat button in the bottom-right corner shows a green
   dot when the assistant is online and a red dot when it
   is not available.

Q: When can I renew my policy?
A: You can renew starting from 30 days before your policy
   expires, and up to 15 days after it expires.

Q: What happens if I try to renew too early?
A: You will see a message letting you know the renewal window
   has not opened yet. Come back closer to your expiry date.

Q: My policy shows "Pending" — what does that mean?
A: Your payment is being processed. This usually resolves
   within a few seconds. Refresh the page and it should
   update to "Active".

Q: I bought a policy but it is not showing in My Policies.
A: Wait a few seconds and refresh the page. If it still does
   not appear, raise a Support Ticket with your payment
   reference number.
